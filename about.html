A Little About Us

Who we are.

Namaste! My name is Sanchit and I manage India Spice with my wife Soma. We are originally from New Delhi and emigrated to Ireland in 2004. After visiting Ireland for the first time in 2001, we instantly feel in love with the energy of the Emerald Isle. A short 3 years later and we emigrated and not long after that, India Spice was established.


What we are about.

Love. Family. Friends. Quality time. Whether it is for a short brunch or an extravagant family gathering, we want every occasion to be as good as the last. And at India Spice, we import the finest herbs, salts and spices to ensure the most authentic dining experience every time. Sorry but the recipies are a family secret!


What we provide.

Whether you want to dine with 20 or dance with 50, you bring the party and India Spice can bring it to life! Our in house band is performs every weekend and is also available for reservations. Or maybe you fancy a home-do? We can bring India to you! Get in touch through the Contact page and leave the rest up to us.
